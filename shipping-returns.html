Shipping & Returns

1. Domestic Shipping

We ship across India. All domestic orders are processed and shipped within 3–5 business days. We offer free shipping on orders above ₹50,000. For orders under ₹50,000, a flat shipping fee of ₹999 will be applied at checkout.

2. International Shipping

International shipping is coming soon. We are working diligently to partner with global couriers to safely deliver Aalfaz jewelry worldwide.

3. Returns

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. The item must be in its original, unworn condition with all original tags and packaging intact. To initiate a return, please contact us at support@alfaaz.com with your order number.

4. Exchanges

Exchanges are available within 7 days of delivery, subject to product availability. Please contact our support team to arrange an exchange.

5. Non-Returnable Items

Custom, engraved, or personalized pieces are made specifically for you and are non-returnable. Items purchased on sale are also considered final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged.

6. Damaged / Wrong Item

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Include clear photo evidence of the issue and your order number so we can quickly resolve it for you.
