Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Aalfaz website or make a purchase, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal details: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address provided at checkout.
  • Payment information: We do not store raw card data. Payments are processed securely via our payment partners (Razorpay).
  • Usage data: Pages you visit, time spent, browser type, and device information, collected automatically via cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders.
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates via email or WhatsApp.
  • Improve the website experience and product offerings.
  • Respond to customer inquiries and support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Processors

To operate our website and process payments, we share data with trusted third-party service providers, including:

  • Razorpay — For processing online payments (INR).
  • Google Firebase — For website hosting and backend database services.
  • Google Analytics — For anonymous website traffic analysis.

Each of these services has their own privacy policy and we encourage you to review them.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your shopping cart session, remember preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; however, some features of the website may not function correctly without them.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for a period of 2 years from the date of your last transaction or interaction with us, after which it is securely deleted from our systems unless required by law to retain it longer.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@alfaaz.com.

7. Contact for Data Requests

For all data-related queries, requests, or concerns, please reach out to our Data Officer at privacy@alfaaz.com. We aim to respond to all requests within 30 days.
